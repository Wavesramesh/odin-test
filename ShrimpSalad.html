Summer Grilled Shrimp Salad

Summer Grilled Shrimp Salad:
I love to make main-dish salads in the summertime and serve them with
some crusty bread and nice glass of wine! this one is one of my favorites,
featuring sweet and smoky grilled shrimp and a cilantro vinaigrette.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2¼ teaspoon smokehouse maple seasoning (such as McCormick Grill mates)
  • 1½ teaspoon lemon juice
  • 12 ounce peeled and deveined shrimp

Cilantro Vinaigrette:

  • ¼ cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablesspoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons chopped cilantro
  • 1 tablesspoon balsamic vinegar
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Salad:

  • 4 cups mixed salad greens, or more to taste
  • ¼ cup thinly sliced English cumcumber
  • ½ cup freshly cooked corn
  • ¼ cup sliced red onion
  • 1 avocado, diced
Direction
  1. Combine olive oil, maple seasoning, and lemon juice in a glass bowl.
    add shrimp and toss to coat. Refrigerate until ready to grill.
  2. Whisk olive oil, honey, lime juice, cilantro, balsamic vinegar, salt,
    and pepper together in a asmall bowl. Set vinagrette aside.
  3. Preheat an indoor or outdoor grill to medium-high heat. Thread the
    shrimp onto skewers. Grill until shrimp turn pink and opaque,
    about 2 minutes per side, remove from skewers and set aside
  4. Place mixed greens in the bottom of large salad bowl. Lay cucumber,
    corn, tomato, red onion, and avocado in section on top of greens, pile
    the grilled shrimp in the center of salad. drizzle with the vinagrette
    and to coat. serve immediately
